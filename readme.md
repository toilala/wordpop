# WordPop

A fast, no-nonsense dictionary for people who read a lot and want definitions without the hassle of tabs. No AI, no tracking, only definitions.


Most dictionary extensions are bloated, slow, or try to sell you a "Pro" plan. I had a hard time finding an extension that fit my needs; a simple, instant popup without any hassle. WordPop just checks my needs—nothing more.

- Uses DictionaryAPI for words, Wikipedia for entities (like "New York City"), and Google Translate as a fallback for phrases.

- Saves lookups in your browser cache. Next time you see the word, it loads in **0ms**.

- No accounts. No tracking. No "AI" processing your data.
